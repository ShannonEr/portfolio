ABOUT

Throughout my academic journey, I explored multiple areas of game development both independently and through team projects. I’ve designed gameplay mechanics and level progression across various genres such as platformers and visual novels, iterating and polishing ideas while collaborating with artists and fellow programmers to ensure each project aligns with shared goals. Professionally, I’ve interned at FallenCranberry Studios and at Nanyang Technological University’s Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information (WKWSCI). I’ve also contributed to the modding community, creating quality‑of‑life mods such as a customizable starting‑currency mod for Lethal Company and an item expansion of special tools for Minecraft. These experiences deepened my understanding of player interaction in both game and research contexts and broadened my perspective on interactive systems.
