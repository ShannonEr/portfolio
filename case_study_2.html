Design Intent

  • To create a prototype of a traditional 2D PVE fighting game. This serves as a mockup for when the game is recreated and polished on a custom engine.

Constraints

  • Time was a strong, primary constraint. While juggling other academic modules, I had to research how to create a fighting game. There were no Unity specific tutorials online on how I would create a pure traditional punch-kick type of game, which meant that I would have to observe and learn through practical experience. This was punishing for me as I had to pick up fast on what was a key feature I needed such as hitstop and the ability to chain moves and output a prototype for my team in just two months. I had to define and design the systems needed from pure visuals and videos, then realize these features in the game. Because of this, I had to be deliberate defining our prototype gameplay scope, focusing on the key mechanics such as attack chaining, and pouring much more of my time into ensuring what would have the biggest impact on feedback loop to ensure a satisfying player experience.
