Overview

  • Project: Color Loops

  • Role: Unity3D Game Programmer, Game Designer

  • Teammates: None

  • Duration: 2-3 Months

  • Date: 2023

  • Game Engine: Unity

Design Intent

  • The player has to escape the castle they were held in. Armed with magical fire that launches from a gun, player can utilize this to aid in escaping. Player can switch between red, blue, and yellow fires, mixing to form secondary colors that have different interactions with the environment.

Constraints

  • Knowledge was a strong, primary constraint. This was one of my earliest foray into Unity. I had to research and learn a plethora of systems.
