Design Intent

  • The player has her soul torn by Hecate after accidentally interrupting a magical ritual. To retrieve her body, she must journey through Hecate's domain, solving puzzles and facing off against the player's corrupted body. My main job was to work on the scene where the player first encounters Hecate. I was also responsible for implementing the sound manager and playtesting levels. This game was also showcased in the final milestone examples 2025 video of my professor.

Constraints

  • There were no strong constraints. Time was a factor but we balanced it well. As this was one of the projects in my first year of university, we had to learn to work and familiarise to work as a team. Thankfully we meshed well with us splitting up our tasks well, ensuring managed to finish our tasks on time. As the fruit of our labour, this game was also featured briefly in our professor's video(https://youtu.be/Z3GWtYCzvpM?si=2sy3xOkOST2CY0Jx) of 2025 game examples to showcase to the future cohorts.
